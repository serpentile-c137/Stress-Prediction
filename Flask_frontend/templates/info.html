Parameter Information Page

EDA Data from the electrodermal activity sensor expressed as microsiemens (μS).
TEMP Data from temperature sensor expressed degrees on the Celsius (°C) scale.
BVP Data from photoplethysmograph. It is derived from the combination of two different measures of the amount of light that is reflected and goes back to the sensor.
HR Average or instantanious heart rate extracted from any heart rate sensing device expressed in Hz.
